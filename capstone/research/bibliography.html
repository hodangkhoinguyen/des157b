Shelton, Suan. "The Davis Centennial Seal." Susan Shelton Ceramic Artist, https://susanshelton.com/Seals/Davis-Centennial-Seal. Accessed 9 May 2023.

Susan Shelton made this web page to introduce about the Davis Centennial Seal which was designed by her. This seal becomes iconic for Davis City because of honoring and highlighting several historic figures and events. On this webpage, Shelton described her overall design and every major fragments of the seal including the central image, the rings, and the ten panels around the seal. One of the panels is about the Innovative Contributions of Citizens which has a picture about biking in Davis. This aspect is very typical in Davis, and this is the topic that I want to do for the capstone project.
